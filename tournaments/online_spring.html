21 March – 3 May Open water – any location

Online Format

Online competitions are not held at a single venue. Each participant fishes wherever they choose — any water, any time of day — within the specified dates. There is no common start, no fixed location. You submit your catch results according to the rules, and the results are compared across all participants.
