Vansjø 30

"Catch the biggest. Win it all."

23 - 24 May Lake Vansjø

About the Venue

Lake Vansjø is an extensive, complex water system in the Østfold region, near Moss and Fredrikstad, offering a challenging venue with great sporting potential. The lake is divided into several basins with different characteristics — from shallow, heavily vegetated bays to deeper sections with distinct bottom structure.

Characteristics:

  • Varied structure: reed beds, underwater meadows, drop-offs, hard flats and channels between islands.
  • Depth: key areas are vegetation edges and hard/soft bottom transitions.
  • Weather conditions: strong exposure to wind.

Venue and Base

The competition takes place on the entire lake Vansjø except for Vestre Vansjø naturreservat.
