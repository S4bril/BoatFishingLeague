About Boat Fishing League

The tournament organizers are anglers with years of experience, for whom fishing is a true passion. They have been honing their skills for years, learning new techniques and spending every free moment on the water. Out of their love for fishing, they organize competitions that are not only about sporting rivalry, but above all bring together people with shared interests.

The organizers' goal is to create a unique atmosphere where both experienced competitors and beginners can share experiences, meet new people, and together enjoy the excitement of competition. The organizers value healthy rivalry, fair play, and good fun — which is why every event becomes an unforgettable experience for all participants.

Everything is organized completely free of charge, as the organizers operate as a non-profit. They do it out of passion and a desire to grow the fishing community, and the greatest satisfaction comes from bringing people together and spending time on the water.
